SIC (Startup Incubation Center)

Incubating your dreams into reality

Why Choose Us ?

Welcome to the Startup Incubator Center at Graphic Era (Hill to be University) [SIC-GEHU], We are a group of tech enthusiasts who love to explore the world of technology and share our knowledge with the world. We have been pivotal in fostering innovation and entrepreneurship. We support startups, helping them overcome challenges, attract talent and investment, and contribute to the local economy. We are proud of our vibrant entrepreneurial community and are committed to supporting more ventures in the future.

Services We Offer

At the Startup Incubation Center (SIC), we are committed to nurturing and empowering startups with comprehensive support and resources. Our range of services is designed to assist entrepreneurs at every stage of their startup journey, from ideation to scaling. Here's what we offer:

1. Mentorship & Guidance -
Our team of seasoned mentors and industry experts provides personalized guidance to help you refine your business idea, create a solid business plan, and navigate the challenges of starting and growing a business.

2. Networking Opportunities-
We offer a dynamic environment where startups can connect with potential investors, industry leaders, and other entrepreneurs. Our networking events, workshops, and pitch sessions open doors to valuable partnerships and funding opportunities.

3. Incubation & Acceleration Programs-
We provide incubation and acceleration programs tailored to your startup's needs. From initial idea validation to market entry and scaling, our programs offer structured guidance to accelerate your growth.
