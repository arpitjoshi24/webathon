Our incubation center provides a range of services, including mentorship, access to funding opportunities, networking events, office space, and workshops on business development, marketing, and legal compliance.

To apply for incubation, you need to submit a detailed business proposal outlining your startup idea, market analysis, and business model. Our selection committee will review your application and contact you for further discussion

Our incubation program may have a nominal fee or equity stake, depending on the services offered and the stage of the startup. We encourage interested entrepreneurs to reach out for detailed information on the fee structure.

The typical duration of our incubation program is 6 to 12 months, during which startups receive support and resources to develop their products and grow their businesses. However, this can vary depending on the specific needs of the startup.
