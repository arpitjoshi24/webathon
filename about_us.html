About Us

Welcome to the Startup Incubator Center at Graphic Era (Hill University) [SIC-GEHU], where innovation meets opportunity. We are a dynamic group of tech enthusiasts driven by a shared passion for exploring the ever-evolving world of technology and spreading that knowledge to the wider community. As a pivotal force in fostering innovation and entrepreneurship, we provide startups with the support they need to navigate the complexities of the business world. Our incubator offers a comprehensive ecosystem that helps startups overcome challenges, from securing funding to scaling operations. We connect entrepreneurs with mentors, provide access to cutting-edge resources, and foster collaboration among creative minds. By facilitating access to talent and investment opportunities, we aim to drive growth and innovation in local and global economies alike. At SIC-GEHU, we take pride in our vibrant entrepreneurial community, which thrives on creativity, collaboration, and relentless pursuit of success. Whether you're a student with a groundbreaking idea or an established entrepreneur looking for support, our incubator is the perfect launchpad for your journey. We are committed to nurturing the next generation of innovators and are excited to continue empowering ventures that will shape the future.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a thriving entrepreneurial ecosystem in Uttarakhand by focusing on four key areas: Skill Development & Internships, Innovation & Incubation Support, Sustainable Practices & Economic Growth, and Community Building and Networking. By concentrating on these pillars, we contribute to the region's economic growth and prosperity. Our vision is to be the leading force for skill development, innovation, and sustainability in Uttarakhand. We empower entrepreneurs with skills and resources to turn ideas into successful ventures. By fostering continuous learning and innovation, we aim to drive economic growth, promote sustainable practices, and create a vibrant entrepreneurial ecosystem for a prosperous future.
